Each type of IRI can be appear more than once. In that case, the user-agent can choose what to use.
Types:
isbn:
ISBN-10 or ISBN-13 (International Standard Book Number; ISO 2108:2005).
issn:
ISSN (International Standard Serial Number; ISO 3297:2007).
mlid:
Minerva Local IDentifier. The URI must be same as "canoninal" property.
msid:
Minerva standard IDentifier. The URI is controlled by Minerva development team.
